Chapter 3: A Moment Alone

« Previous Next »

"Kunikida, can you step into my office please?" Fukuzawa asked, raising an eyebrow at Ranpo and Dazai. "Of course, I am coming right now." Kunikida said with a nod.

Kunikida sat down in one of chairs, holding his notebook close to his chest. He shook his head, trying to relax after what had happened earlier. He would have thanked Fukuzawa for saving him but then he would also have to explain why he was saying that, and well, he was not in the mood to express how much of a major crush he had on the president.

Fukuzawa looked at his door, and decide to lock it, after all, they other's were very nosy at times. "I made some tea... if we can't get it in the cafe, this works too... right?" he approached Kunikida slowly, he finally removed the jacket from around his shoulders and placed it on the chair. "Are you alright?"

He nodded, smiling up at him, "It's perfectly fine, thank you for making tea." he mumbled, suddenly feeling rather nervous. "I am... fine, everything is alright. Dazai and Ranpo were, just teasing me, it's nothing." he mumbled. "Do you need help with the tea?" he asked.

Fukuzawa nodded his head at the other and let out a small smile, "it is the only way I could thank you for earlier.." he picked the tea pot and poured it into the cups. "Hmm.. I am rather fine.. no need to worry about me.." he picked up the two cups, handing one to Kunikida, "Come.. sit here... the chairs are for buisness.." he walked over to the small couch

Kunikida stood up with a nervous smile, holding the cup and being careful not to drop it, he was trying his best to remain calm. "Oh... yes of course." he whispered, walking over to the couch and sitting down. "And there is no need to thank me, really." his smile slightly widened. He started blowing on his hot tea.

Fukuzawa shook his head "Your kindness is very sweet Kunikida.." he sipped his tea. he flinched a little, but he played it off cool though. "Mmm..." he looked down, and then at Kunikida. "You.. seem nervous." he commented.

He finally took a sip of his tea, closing his eyes and enjoying the taste and aroma. Kunikida took a deep breath, finally relaxing a bit into the couch. "No, no I... I am alright. I feel much better now." he answered truthfully. "The tea is very good." he said with a smile, "It's what I needed after such an eventful day." Kunikida offered him a shy smile, his face was slightly red.

He kept watching Kunikida with a great curiosity. "I am glad.. I know the others could be stressful.. so feel free to drop in here anytime for some tea.. or just to get away..." he took a sip of his tea, it was finally good to drink. "Quite the.. eventful day.." he looked ahead and smiled a bit, a bit of pink creeping up on his face.

"That's very... sweet of you, thank you." Kunikida could feel his face heating up even more, he tried to hide it behind his cup as he took another sip. "It was, but, I had a really good time." he whispered, staring at him. He didn't know what to do, but he felt like his expression was able to say more than he wanted. He wanted to reach forward to take his hand, but he felt like that would be too forward, after all, Kunikida didn't even know if he had suspected anything.

Fukuzawa turned his head towards Kunikida, he dropped his facade completely, closing his eyes and smiling towards the other, without shying away. "I do wish... I spent more time with the cats though...ah well" he sighed and looked away. He leaned back on the couch, slightly leaning towards the other.

Kunikida thought for a bit, and he opened his mouth to say something, looking a little hesitant. "Maybe... one of these days, we could go back there, and you wouldn't have to go there alone this time." he proposed, he leaned a bit towards him as well. "If you want of course, but since I know you like cats I thought that, it would be a good idea." he took another sip of his tea.

"That is a nice thought...but I do not wish for enemies to remember my paths... I rather not risk innocent lives... as much,.. as I love... tiny... fluffy.... kittens..." his face went slightly redder. "As.. the president.. I need to ensure safety of everyone.. in and out of the agency.." he sighed, and leaned on Kunikida's shoulder fully, with his eyes closed.

"Maybe we could go after a while, so that no-one will notice any pattern." Kunikida suggested. "That's so responsible of you..." he said softly, not moving away. He had noticed that Fukuzawa was leaning on his shoulder, but at first he thought he was imagining things, then, it was way too difficult for him to process it, so he simply... stood still, still smiling.

Fukuzawa opened his eyes a little at that 'responsible' he sighed a bit, before closing his eyes again. "I do try... and I will.. think about it,, Kunikida." he sipped his tea. He enjoyed the silence for what it was worth, simply listening to Kunikida's breath, and matching it. "I should let you return to work.." he sighed, and lifted his head off Kunikida.

Kunikida's eyes widened, he could sense that he had lifted his head off him now, had he done something wrong? Maybe he had expected him to do something as well? He was so awkward, he truly had no experience in these things. Kunikida had always been more focused on his work so he'd never really learned how to truly express himself. He shook his head, slowly and hesitantly placing his arm around his waist, his chest felt like it would burst. "Don't worry I... am alright. I don't have much work left." he mumbled, taking another sip of his tea.

Fukuzawa finished the rest of his tea in one sip and put it on the arm rest of the couch, before he felt a hand go around his waist. He looked down a little before looking over at Kunikida. "Very well then.. feel free to stay here as long as you'd like" He bowed his head slightly, before resting his head on Kunikida's shoulder again.

That helped him calm down a bit, and Kunikida relaxed back on the couch again as his arm was still around Fukuzawa's waist, he smiled as he felt his head resting on his shoulder once again. "Thank you..." he said softly, "I hope I... am not distracting you from your own work." he couldn't believe that this was not a dream. It was felt so nice to be real.

Fukuzawa opened his eyes and looked over at his desk, "I have.. some work.. but I don't necessarily feel like doing it currently.. so it can wait.." He closed his eyes once more, "It is. mostly letter writing.. and I do not enjoy that.. Ranpo does the best job... he speaks what I wish I could say..." he explained, he moved his hands up to put them in his sleeves.

Kunikida nodded, fully understanding what he meant. "I see..." he mumbled, "He is truly a very gifted kid." a soft smile appeared on his face, "And he doesn't even have an ability, it's... outstanding and admirable how he does all these things perfectly, on his own." he made himself a bit more comfortable and at some point, he placed his own head on top of his, smiling sweetly. The President smelt so nice.

Fukuzawa nodded a little, "He never needed an ability.. just to believe he had one in the first place.." he hummed, "Hmm.. well he is such a fast learner... the changes through those years.. I am surprised..." he smiled to himself a bit more. "I am glad.. I found him when I did.." he let out a deep breath and opened his eyes, he turned his head slightly, wanting to look up at Kunikida. "You seem.. at peace.."

He smiled as he heard all of that about Ranpo, it was obvious and it had already been, that Fukuzawa cared about Ranpo a lot, he treated him like his own son.
